Chapter 2 Genocide Theory


Overview

Genocide Theory Chapter 1's Ending Cutscene;
The one that inspired the theory.[2]

After the events of Chapter 1, a cutscene plays at the end, where Kris convulses and jerks out of bed, slouching towards the center of their room, pulling out their knife, staring at the player, and grinning, cutting to the credits immediately after. This is the cutscene that sparked one of the very first theories in the fandom - the “Chapter 2 is Kris going on the Genocide Route” Theory.

Origins

The post-genocide true pacifist endings, both the stay (top) and leave options (bottom).[1]
